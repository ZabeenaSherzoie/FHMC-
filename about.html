The idea for this cinema and website first came up to me from my own movie nights experience with my cousins , It started after the Coronavirus pandemic ,we were at our houses no one went out, we ofthen played in the garden(Football, Tennis, even Hide and Seek ) then we decided to do something we haven't done yet. So I said that I have a great horror movie let's watch it tonight, we agreed and collected some money for snacks . At night we gathered with girls and watched two movie and spent joyful time together until the morning and we decide to do it once a while and all those nights were unique. There was even nights in which we watched movies until 4:00 am and then went out together for a walk with loosing energy and joy. They became the most memorable part of our lives. So we wanted to share the fun and joy of these moments with others.
And here we are with this website

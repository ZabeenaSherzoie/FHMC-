Friends' Horror Movie Cinema

The idea for this cinema and website first came up to me from my own movie nights experience with my cousins , It started after the Coronavirus pandemic ,we were at our houses no one went out, we ofthen played in the garden(Football, Tennis, even Hide and Seek ) then we decided to do something we haven't done yet. So I said that I have a great horror movie let's watch it tonight, we agreed and collected some money for snacks . At night we went to my uncle's house with girls and watched two movie and spent joyful time together until the morning and we decide to do it once a while and all those nights were unique. They became the most memorable part of our lives. So now we are here to provide you the opportunity to experience a great joy too!

So let's take a look to those memories here!
